Noodle


['nu:dl] | (noun)

1. Người ngớ ngẩn, người xuẩn ngốc, người khờ dại
2. (số nhiểu) mì sợi

Ví dụ:

  • instant noodles. Dịch
  • We have rice noodle soup with beef. Dịch
  • Chinese food is often served with rice or noodles. Dịch
  • chicken noodle soup. Dịch
  • I had breakfast with instant noodle. Dịch
